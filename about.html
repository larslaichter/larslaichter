Short Bio

My primary interests lie in the intersections of philosophy of mind and philosophy of computation. I am currently pursuing a personalized BA&Sc major at Quest University Canada on the question ‘What are the limists of the mind?’. I have attended the Budapest Semester in Cognitive Science in spring 2017.   My research aspirations revolve around the fundamental properties of the computational metaphor in cognitive science and topics in the dynamic account of cognition.
