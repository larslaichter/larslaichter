Short Bio

My name is Lars Š. Laichter and I hold a BA&Sc with a major in philosophy from Quest University Canada. My research primarily revolves around the question "What are the limits of the mind?" which I examine through the lens of philosophy of mind and philosophy of artificial intelligence. In my honours thesis, I analyzed the illusionist account of consciousness. During my undergraduate studies, I have attended the Budapest Semester in Cognitive Science and collaborated with researchers from the University of Sussex and the Eötvös Loránd University.
